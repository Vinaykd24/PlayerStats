PRITHVI EDIFICE WHITE COPPER INTER IT CHAMPIONSHIP 2016-17

TCS Cricket Team after winning the Pratham Sports IT Cup and the REGREEN Corporate Championship has now finished as runners-up in the PRITHVI EDIFICE WHITE COPPER INTER IT CHAMPIONSHIP 2017 played at Nehru Stadium Pune. This tournament was played amongst the top 32 IT cricket teams.

TCS played seven matches in the tourney and won all its matches before the Final Game. In the league stages TCS lashed teams Maersk, Tieto and JCI comfortably to enter into the knock out stages of the tournament. In the pre quarter finals TCS won against Amdocs comfortably moving into the quarter finals. In the quarter final TCS beat SunGard-FIS with a big margin. Semifinal was played against TCS and Cybage where TCS outplayed the opponent by 37 runs to secure a place in the finals of the competition. The final was an exciting encounter between TCS and Infosys. Both the teams put up their best show where TCS lost the match in the end.

TCS entire team played exceptionally well and showed great skills with both bat & ball throughout the tourney which made this victory possible. There were some outstanding individual performances in the Mayank Jasore was adjudged as the Best Batsman for his exceptional performances with Bat scoring 321 runs in the tournament. Gaurav Singh was awarded as the Best bowler of the tournament taking 13 wickets in the championship. Vikram Randhawa showed great batting skills andreceived Man of the Match award in various matches in the tournament. Sunil Babar, Soumya Mohanty, Mohammad Saleem alsomade some vital contributions in different matches.

Winner of Regreen T20 Corporate Cricket Championship 2017

TCS Cricket Team crowned as Champion in the corporate cricket after winning the REGREEN T20 CRICKET CHAMPIONSHIP 2017 played in Marunji Pune. This tournament played against the top 16 corporate teams in the vicinity. TCS was outstanding throughout and was the only undefeated side in the entire tourney. In the league stages TCS trashed teams like Poona CA, Hives Lab and Tranter comfortably to enter into knock out stages of the tournament. In the Quarter final TCS won against Poona CA once again with a huge margin to register a place into the Semi Finals. TCS with the brilliant performances defeated Regreen to secure a place into the Final of the competition. The final match is played between TCS and Aspire Home Finace Ltd. at Mulashi Cricket Ground in Pune. TCS outplayed Aspire in all the departments. Mayank Jasore's century in the final was the highlight of the game and took the game from the opposition team after the first inning. TCS after posting target of 179 Runs bowled exceptionally well and bowled out Aspire for mere 91 Runs. In the bowling department Rahul Garg took a 5 wickets haul which was backed up with some brilliant fielding by Soumya Mohanty taking 5 catches in a single match.

TCS entire team played exceptionally well and showed great skills with both bat and ball throughout the tourney which made this victory possible. There were some outstanding individual performances in the tournament.

Mayank Jasore was adjudged as the Best Batsman for his exceptional performance with Bat scoring 304 runs in 3 innings with included two unbeaten centuries (148* and 121*)

Soumya Mohanti awarded as Best Fielder of the tournament. He took 10 brilliant catches in the championship and scored (102 and 54)

Rahul Garg, Soumya Mohanti and Mayank Jasore received Man of the Match awards in the various matches in this tournament.

Vikram Randhawa is top run getters in the competition. Shantanu Nadkarni, Rutuparn Bhave, Hrishikesh Udale and Anand Mishra made some vital contributions in different matches.
